About Aavin

Aavin is the Tamil Nadu Cooperative Milk Producers’ Federation Limited, delivering high-quality dairy products to millions of households across Tamil Nadu.

  • Over 30 years of service in the dairy industry
  • Trusted by millions of customers
  • Providing pure and nutritious milk products
  • Supporting thousands of dairy farmers

Our Services

Milk Distribution

Daily supply of fresh, pure milk to homes, shops, and institutions across Tamil Nadu.

Dairy Product Sales

A wide range of products like curd, ghee, paneer, butter, ice cream, and flavored milk.

Bulk Supply to Institutions

Supplying milk and milk-based products to schools, hospitals, hotels, and government programs.

Farmer Support Services

Empowering rural dairy farmers with training, veterinary support, and fair pricing.

Cold Chain Logistics

Ensuring freshness with advanced chilling centers and refrigerated vehicles.

Retail Outlets

Access our dairy products at thousands of Aavin parlours and franchise stores.
